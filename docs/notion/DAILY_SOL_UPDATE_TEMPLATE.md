# Daily Sheet of Life Update Template

Use this template once per day to report what happened and what should change in Sheet of Life.

You can paste the filled-out version into Codex or ChatGPT with:

```text
Use C:\Users\Clark\OneDrive\Documents\Sheet of Life\CHATGPT_NOTION_UPDATE_GUIDE.md and update my Sheet of Life Notion setup from this daily update:
```
